About FactSense

Built for the
Future of Industry

FactSense is an end-to-end Industrial IoT monitoring platform designed to give engineers and plant managers real-time insight into the operational status of every machine on the floor.

From temperature and vibration to gas levels and electrical current — every sensor is connected, every anomaly is caught, and every decision is backed by live data.

Our intelligent dashboard leverages multi-sensor correlation to detect patterns, allowing you to move beyond reactive repairs and proactively plan maintenance schedules.
